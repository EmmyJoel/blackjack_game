How to play

  • Start a round by clicking the START-GAME button
  • You'll be handed two cards of random values
  • The goal is to get a perfect number- 21 (Blackjack!)
  • To keep playing, your total cards shoul not exceed 21
  • You have a chance to pick a new card as long as your cards equates below 21.
  • Get a blackjack with the fewest clicks possible
